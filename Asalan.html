About Us

Nestled in the ancient vineyards of Armenia, Asalan Wine brings you a legacy of 6,000 years of winemaking mastery. Our family estate combines volcanic soil, high-altitude sun, and artisanal techniques to craft wines that echo the soul of the Caucasus.

Every bottle tells a story — from hand-harvested Areni grapes to patient aging in oak barrels. We honor tradition while embracing elegance, delivering a truly premium experience for connoisseurs worldwide.
